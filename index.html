Trip Cost Calculator

Internal tool — calculate, review and export trip costing PDFs with full breakdowns.

📋 Trip Basics
Set the trip name, duration and group composition.
👶 Children (Age-based Lodging Pricing)
Hotel/homestay charges depend on child age. Add each child separately.
Lodging Age Rules:
🟢 Below 3 years — No hotel/lodging charge (complimentary)
🟡 3 to 6 years — Half the per-person lodging/combined rate
🔵 Above 6 years — Full per-person rate (same as adult for hotel)

Food cost for children: enter adult food rates in the Food & Lodging table — adjust manually if children eat differently.

🍽️ Food & Lodging — Per Day Breakdown
Enter per-adult rates. Child lodging is auto-calculated based on age above. Days = Nights + 1.
Day Food Only (₹/adult) Lodging Only (₹/adult) Food+Lodging Combined (₹/adult) Use Combined? Copy Forward

Check "Use Combined?" when a property bundles food & lodging. Use "Copy Forward" to apply the same rates to the next N days. Child lodging is auto-applied based on age rules.

🚗 Transport — Per Day (Multiple Vehicles)
Add one or more vehicles per day. E.g. one Bolero + one small car. Each vehicle has its own cost and route note.
🏨 Property Selector (B2B Rates)
Select properties used in this trip. B2B cost is added automatically. Skip if you've filled lodging manually above.
➕ Extras & Miscellaneous
Permits, entry fees, guide charges, tips, etc.
📈 Markup & Profit
Apply a percentage markup over total base cost to get the selling price.
